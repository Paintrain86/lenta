О LENTA TRADE

Lenta Trade is a Business to Business (B2B) marketplace for food resellers. We are based in Saint Petersburg from where our trade platform is monitored and managed. Lenta Trade is a part of Lenta LLC with an extended store network of more than 180 hypermarkets in more than 75 cities, 7 DC’s and 6 regional offices across Russia. Read more
